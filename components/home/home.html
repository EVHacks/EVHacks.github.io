WHAT’S A HACKATHON?

Hackathons provide a venue for self-expression and creativity through technology. People with technical backgrounds come together, form teams around a problem or idea, and collaboratively code a unique solution from scratch — these generally take shape in the form of websites, mobile apps, and robots.

Dave Fontenot, founder of MHacks

FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

What if I don’t know how to code?

You do not need to know how to code! We will be hosting a variety of workshops throughout the day such as making your own website from scratch using html/css and more advanced workshops. In addition, we will have mentors, both on-site and virtually.

Who can participate at this event?

This hackathon is open to high school students only. If you already graduated from high school and you would like to be a mentor, please fill out the mentor sign up form and we will reach out to you shortly.

How much does this event cost?

Thanks to our generous sponsors and support from our school’s administration, admission is free! We will provide you WiFi, meals, swag, and workspace.

How do I form or join a team?

It’s best to form a team prior to the hackathon since we’ll only have around 11+ hours to hack; utilize the Facebook events page to find your future hack buddies!

What else should I do to prep for the hackathon?

Get plenty of sleep the night before, shower and use deodorant. Steeping in a room with 150+ people for 12 hours can get gnarly, so keep yourself clean!
